Thunder Cats Secure Win Against Braddock Bearcats

By GameChanger Media

Thunder Cats bested Braddock Bearcats 6-1 on Wednesday at Lake Braddock Secondary School.

Thunder Cats got on the board in the first inning after Ian singled, scoring one run.

Dylan started on the hill for Thunder Cats. The right-handed pitcher allowed one hit and zero runs over one inning, striking out two and walking none. #3 led things off on the mound for Braddock Bearcats.
